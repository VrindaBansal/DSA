<Intuition>
BFS and DFS are the same five lines of code. The only difference is the
container holding the frontier: a **queue** explores in rings (oldest
first), a **stack** dives down corridors (newest first). The visual runs
both on the identical graph simultaneously — watching the two frontiers
diverge teaches more than any prose. This is npm resolving your dependency
order, Maps flooding outward from your location, and "people you may know"
at distance two.
</Intuition>
