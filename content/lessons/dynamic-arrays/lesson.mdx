<Intuition>
The array is the structure the hardware actually wants: contiguous memory,
index arithmetic, cache lines full of your data. Every technique in this
module — two pointers, sliding windows, prefix sums — is a way of touching
that contiguous block **once** instead of once per query. Watch the window
below maintain its sum with one add and one subtract per slide; that
overlap-reuse move is the heart of half of all easy-to-medium interview
problems.
</Intuition>
